What are your goals?

There are two distinct types of goals- Long Term and Short Term. Each type has a specific purpose and traits.

Long-Term Goal:

A long term goal is one that defines future plans. Long-term goals are typically viewed as goals that require a longer period of time to complete and may stretch over several years. These type of goals pertain to individuals lifestyle dreams,career path and family/relationship ideals. You will need tp set a strategy for accomplishing this type of goal.


Short-Term Goal:

A short-term goal is one that can be accomplished in a shorter period of time. Some may define a short-term goal not by time but as something that can be completed in a single step or task. Short-term goals do not always have to be a step of a long-term goal. These types of goals can be independent actions of achievement.
